# Team Work Contract

This document outlines how our team will work together throughout this project. Our goal is to create a respectful, organized, and supportive working environment where everyone contributes meaningfully and feels heard. By agreeing to this contract, we commit to clear communication, shared responsibility, and professional standards.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Work Distribution

-   Work will be shared fairly among all team members.
-   Every task will have one primary person responsible for leading it.
-   Larger tasks will be divided into smaller parts to ensure balance.
-   We will review each other’s work before submission to maintain quality and support one another.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Expected Work Hours

-   Each member commits to contributing approximately 6–8 hours per week.
-   We will aim to start tasks at least 3 days before deadlines.
-   During milestone or deadline weeks, we understand that additional time may be necessary.
-   Each member is responsible for managing their time and communicating early if they fall behind.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Meetings

-   We will meet once per week.
-   Additional meetings may be scheduled when approaching major deadlines.
-   Attendance is important. If someone cannot attend, they must inform the team in advance and provide an update.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Meeting Agendas and Minutes

-   A meeting agenda will be shared at least 4 hours before each meeting.
-   One team member will take notes during each session.
-   The responsibility for preparing the agenda and recording minutes will rotate weekly.
-   Meeting notes will be stored in a shared document so everyone can stay informed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Style of Working

-   Each meeting will begin with a short progress update from every member.
-   Members will briefly share:
    -   What they have completed
    -   What they are currently working on
    -   Any challenges or blockers
-   Work will be pushed to GitHub regularly to ensure transparency.
-   Major changes will be discussed before merging to avoid confusion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Quality Expectations

-   We aim to maintain high academic and professional standards in all deliverables.
-   Code should be:
    -   Clear and readable
    -   Well-commented
    -   Properly formatted
-   Reports and analyses should include:
    -   Clear explanations
    -   Well-labeled visualizations
    -   Reproducible results
-   We will avoid last-minute submissions without team review.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. Availability

-   Team members will communicate any availability constraints early.
-   Planned absences must be shared at least 24 hours in advance whenever possible.
-   We respect that members may have work, family, or personal commitments.
